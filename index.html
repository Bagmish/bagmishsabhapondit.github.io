Hi! I am a PhD researcher working on the effect of generative soundscapes on sleep in collaboration with Freie Universität Berlin, Donders Centre for Cognitive Neuroimaging and Endel Sound GmbH.

I started with a Bachelors in Chemistry but drawn by the mysteries of the human mind and behavior, I pursued an MA in Psychology from the University of Delhi and subsequently an MSc in Cognitive Science from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Gandhinagar. I am deeply interested in understanding the mysteries of the mind from first principles, using tools from cognitive sciences, neurosciences, computational and systems theory. My primary research interests are consciousness, perception, meditation, and altered states.

Apart from academic research, I also write on Substack to communicate my understanding of the various aspects of the mind to a broader audience.
